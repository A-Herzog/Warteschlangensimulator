The number of clients in service process at a station (or in the system) is the number of clients which are currently being served, i.e. which are not anymore in the queue.

Meaning of the columns in this table:

  • Station: Model station for which the number of clients is displayed in the corresponding row. "System" stands for the number of waiting clients in the entire system.
  • E[NS]: Average number of clients in service process at this station.
  • Std[NS]: Standard deviation of the number of clients in service process at this station.
  • Var[NS]: Variance of the number of clients in service process at this station.
  • CV[NS]: Coefficient of variation of the number of clients in service process at this station.
  • Sk[NS]: Skewness of the number of clients in service process at this station.
  • Kurt[NS]: Excess kurtosis of the number of clients in service process at this station.
  • Min[NS]: Minimum recorded number of clients in service process at this station during the simulation.
  • Max[NS]: Maximum recorded number of clients in service process at this station during the simulation.
  • 10%-Quantil[NS]: 10% quantile of the number of clients in service process at this station during the simulation. 10% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in service process at this station.
  • 25-Quantil[NS]: 25% quantile of the number of clients in service process at this station during the simulation. 25% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in service process at this station.
  • 50%-Quantil[NS]: 50% quantile of the number of clients in service process at this station during the simulation. 50% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in service process at this station. This value is also the median of the number of clients in service process at this station.
  • 75%-Quantil[NS]: 75% quantile of the number of clients in service process at this station during the simulation. 75% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in service process at this station.
  • 90%-Quantil[NS]: 90% quantile of the number of clients in service process at this station during the simulation. 90% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in service process at this station.
