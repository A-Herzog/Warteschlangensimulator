The transport of clients between different transporter start and transport destination stations is done by transporters, which can be organized in several transporter groups.

This page shows, for each group of transporters, how many transporters were working at each time and how often. The average utilization of a transporter group is referred to as rho. In addition, the other usual parameters such as standard deviations, variances, coefficients of variation, skewness and excess kurtosis of the number of busy transporters in the group are also given.

The time share in which all transporters were idle (idle share (all transporters)) and the time share in which all transporters worked simultaneously (full busy share) were also output.
