This page shows how many clients were in the entire system (total and differentiated by client type) and at the individual stations.

A distinction is made between the number of clients in total i.e. waiting, in transport and in service and the number of waiting clients. The abbreviation N is used for the total number, while waiting clients are identified by NQ.

For all values average value, standard deviation, variances, coefficients of variation, skewness and quantiles are displayed.
