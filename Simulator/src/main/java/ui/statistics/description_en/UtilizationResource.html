Clients are served at the process stations by operators, who can be organized in several resource groups. The operators are not necessarily permanently assigned to one process station.

This page shows, for each group of resources, how many operators were working at each time and how often. The average utilization of a resource group is referred to as rho. In addition, the other usual parameters such as standard deviations, variances, coefficients of variation and skewness of the number of busy operators in the group are also given.

The time share in which all operators were idle (idle share (all operators)) and the time share in which all operators worked simultaneously (full busy share) were also output.
