The number of clients at a station (or in the system) is the total number of clients at the station, i.e. no distinction is made between waiting clients and clients in the service process.

Meaning of the columns in this table:

  • Station: Model station for which the number of clients is displayed in the corresponding row. "System" stands for the number of clients in the entire system.
  • E[N]: Average number of clients at this station.
  • Std[N]: Standard deviation of the number of clients at this station.
  • Var[N]: Variance of the number of clients at this station.
  • CV[N]: Coefficient of variation of the number of clients at this station.
  • Sk[N]: Skewness of the number of clients at this station.
  • Min[N]: Minimum recorded number of clients at this station during the simulation.
  • Max[N]: Maximum recorded number of clients at this station during the simulation.
  • 10%-Quantil[N]: 10% quantile of the number of clients at this station during the simulation. 10% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been at this station.
  • 25-Quantil[N]: 25% quantile of the number of clients at this station during the simulation. 25% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been at this station.
  • 50%-Quantil[N]: 50% quantile of the number of clients at this station during the simulation. 50% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been at this station. This value is also the median of the number of clients at this station.
  • 75%-Quantil[N]: 75% quantile of the number of clients at this station during the simulation. 75% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been at this station.
  • 90%-Quantil[N]: 90% quantile of the number of clients at this station during the simulation. 90% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been at this station.
