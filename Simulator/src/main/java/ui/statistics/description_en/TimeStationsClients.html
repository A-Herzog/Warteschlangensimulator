The waiting, transfer and service times are not only displayed for clients on their way through the system, but the stations also measure the time taken by the different types. In the case of a model with only one station, the waiting times across all clients correspond to the waiting times at the station etc. In the case of a model with several process stations, the total waiting times at all process units correspond to the total waiting times collected from all clients. Within a station, the times are recorded in total as well as subdivided again according to client types. This page shows the data within a station divided according to client types.

The average waiting times are designated by E[W], the average transfer times are designated by E[T] and the average service times are designated by E[S].

For all time entries average values, standard deviations, variances, coefficients of variation and quantiles are displayed.
