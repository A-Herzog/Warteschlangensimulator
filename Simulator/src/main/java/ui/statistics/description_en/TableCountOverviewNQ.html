Meaning of the columns in this table:

  • Station: Model station for which the number of clients is displayed in the corresponding row. "System" stands for the number of waiting clients in the entire system.
  • E[NQ]: Average number of clients in the queue at this station.
  • Std[NQ]: Standard deviation of the number of clients in the queue at this station.
  • Var[NQ]: Variance of the number of clients in the queue at this station.
  • CV[NQ]: Coefficient of variation of the number of clients in the queue at this station.
  • Sk[NQ]: Skewness of the number of clients in the queue at this station.
  • Min[NQ]: Minimum recorded number of clients in the queue at this station during the simulation.
  • Max[NQ]: Maximum recorded number of clients in the queue at this station during the simulation.
  • 10%-Quantil[NQ]: 10% quantile of the number of clients in the queue at this station during the simulation. 10% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in the queue at this station.
  • 25-Quantil[NQ]: 25% quantile of the number of clients in the queue at this station during the simulation. 25% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in the queue at this station.
  • 50%-Quantil[NQ]: 50% quantile of the number of clients in the queue at this station during the simulation. 50% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in the queue at this station. This value is also the median of the number of clients in the queue at this station.
  • 75%-Quantil[NQ]: 75% quantile of the number of clients in the queue at this station during the simulation. 75% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in the queue at this station.
  • 90%-Quantil[NQ]: 90% quantile of the number of clients in the queue at this station during the simulation. 90% of the time at most as displayed in this cell clients have been in the queue at this station.
